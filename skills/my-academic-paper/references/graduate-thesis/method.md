# Graduate Thesis Method Rules

1. When applying a method, explicitly critique the limitations of other methods.
2. Every scale, parameter choice, and threshold must have a basis, such as:
   - optimization theory
   - official standards
   - authoritative prior literature
3. If no basis exists, perform sensitivity analysis.
4. The dimensions and components proposed earlier in the theory must stay consistent with later use.
5. Weight settings must not be subjective; determine them through experiments, mathematical methods, or equivalent objective procedures.
