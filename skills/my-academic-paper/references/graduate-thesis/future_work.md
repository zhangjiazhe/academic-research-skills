# Graduate Thesis Future Work Rules

## Position in Workflow

`不足与未来展望` is written after `主要结论` and `主要创新`.

## Core Goal

This section is about research-level limitations and future research directions.
It should begin from the present study and explain what should be studied next.

## Direction Guidance

Good directions include:

1. Research transfer:
   - how the research object can be transferred to similar places or similar objects
   - how transferability can be verified through comparable experiments or applications
2. Engineering practice:
   - how the study can move toward real engineering application
   - what implementation process or procedure would be required
3. Model updating:
   - how the present method can be compared with newer models
   - how data types can be expanded, including multi-source data
   - how a newer model can be introduced under the same research problem
4. Practical operation:
   - give concrete operational paths tied to actual policy, conditions, or practice
   - do not remain vague or generic

## Constraints

1. Do not write speculative future assumptions as if they were already established.
2. The section must stay at the research level.
3. It must not collapse into fieldwork chores such as generic data collection.
4. Do not introduce brand-new core claims absent from the thesis body.
5. Future directions should grow out of this study's actual limitations, extension paths, and application possibilities.
