# Graduate Thesis Innovation Rules

## Position in Workflow

`主要创新` is written after `主要结论` and before `不足与未来展望`.

## Core Goal

`主要创新` should focus on technical innovation in:
- research object
- research method
- solved problem
- achieved effect

Quantified support should be included where possible.

## Recommended Writing Patterns

Use flexibly from the following patterns:

1. One-sentence summary, then expand.
2. `围绕（针对）xx问题，提出/建立了xx，以达到/实现/解决/克服了xx目的。`
3. `针对xxxx问题/不足，提出/建立了xxxx，实现/解决/克服了xxxx。`
4. Multi-part pattern:
   - `针对……问题/不足，提出/建立了……，实现/解决/克服了……。`
   - `对于xxx，建立xx以实现……。`
5. `针对xx问题，本文进行了xx，实现了xx。` Then expand with quantified support.

## Constraints

1. Innovation must remain at the technical level.
2. Innovation items must be traceable to the body chapters.
3. Do not introduce new terms or new results.
4. Do not treat simple method stacking as innovation.
5. A new method is not merely gradual procedural stacking or method accumulation.
6. Avoid inflated wording such as:
   - `一套`
   - `全面`
   - `系统`
7. Keep each innovation item concise, roughly around five lines when rendered.
