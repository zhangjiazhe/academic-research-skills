# Graduate Thesis Conclusion Rules

## Position in Workflow

After all regular chapters are complete, write the final chapter in this order:

1. `主要结论`
2. `主要创新`
3. `不足与未来展望`

## Writing Goal

`主要结论` must look back from the perspective of a completed study.
It should refine, not repeat at length, the detailed body content.

## Recommended Writing Pattern

Use a structure such as:

`围绕xx问题（大问题），本文构建了xx，形成了xx（目的）。得到的主要结论有：`

`（1）一句话总结 + 定量结果`

`（2）……`

## Core Constraints

1. The body chapters are detailed; the conclusion must be refined and concise.
2. The conclusion must look back on:
   - the concrete technical route
   - the research results
   - the conclusions
   - the achieved outcomes
3. The Introduction is only a concise forward-looking line of thought; `主要结论` may explicitly refer back to the concrete technical route because the work is now complete.
4. Do not introduce new terms or new results.
5. The conclusion must summarize only what already appears in the body text.
6. Quantitative engineering-oriented statements may be approximate instead of over-precise where precision would be misleading in practical use.
7. Self-evaluation must remain restrained.
8. Method sections do not self-evaluate, but the conclusion may use wording such as `有效` or `准确` when clearly supported.
9. Keep each conclusion item concise, roughly around five lines when rendered.
