# Graduate Thesis Workflow Rules

## Whole-Thesis Workflow

1. The whole thesis must continuously obey the same global rule groups:
   - title rules
   - reference rules
   - formatting rules
   - figure / table / formula rules
   - citation-placement rules
   - wording rules
2. Reference rules and citation-placement rules must be used together.
3. Write chapter by chapter.
4. Start from Chapter 2.
5. Continue chapter by chapter until the conclusion chapter is finished.
6. Write the Introduction last, after all body chapters and the conclusion chapter are complete.

## Regular Chapter Structure

Each regular chapter has three structural parts:

1. `章前言`
2. 正文
3. `章小结`

## Actual Writing Order for Regular Chapters

Write each regular chapter in this order:

1. 正文
2. `章小结`
3. `章前言`

## Body Rules

1. The 正文 may contain as many subsections as the research content or workload requires.
2. The number of subsections is not fixed.
3. For each subsection inside the 正文, use this exact order:
   1. `节前言`
   2. Method
   3. Results
   4. Discussion

## Conclusion Chapter Workflow

For the final chapter, use this exact order:

1. `主要结论`
2. `主要创新`
3. `不足与未来展望`

## Introduction Workflow

After all previous chapters and the conclusion chapter are complete:

1. Use `引言` to draft the Introduction
2. Build the review on the basis of the already completed thesis content
3. When writing the literature review, coordinate reference rules and citation-placement rules
