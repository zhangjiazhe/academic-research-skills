# Graduate Thesis Chapter Summary Rules

1. `章小结` is written after the whole chapter body is complete.
2. Write from the completed-study perspective.
3. Recommended pattern:
   - `针对xx，进行了xx。`
   - `主要结论有：`
   - `（1）一句话总结 + 定量结果`
   - `（2）……`
4. The chapter summary must condense the detailed chapter body into refined conclusions.
5. The summary should cover:
   - technical route in concise form
   - research results
   - conclusions
   - achieved outcomes
6. Keep it concise, around five lines when rendered.
7. When giving quantitative conclusions related to engineering practice, avoid overly precise values; use approximate wording such as `大概` or `大约` when necessary.
8. Self-evaluation should remain restrained.
9. Method sections should not self-evaluate; chapter summaries may use restrained wording such as `有效` or `较为准确` when supported.
10. Do not introduce new terms or new results.
11. The summary must only refine what already appears in the chapter body.
