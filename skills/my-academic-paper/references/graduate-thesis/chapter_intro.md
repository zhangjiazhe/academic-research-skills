# Graduate Thesis Chapter Introduction Rules

1. `章前言` is written after the chapter body and `章小结`.
2. Use one paragraph if possible; do not split it into two paragraphs.
3. The paragraph should:
   - identify the problem in one sentence
   - state that, based on this, this chapter conducts or proposes xx
   - state the upcoming research aspects in a compact sequence such as `首先……，其次……`
   - end with one sentence of significance
4. It should answer:
   - why this chapter studies the problem
   - from which aspects the chapter will proceed
   - what target the chapter aims to reach
5. Do not include process-heavy narration.
6. Do not write a separate conclusion paragraph inside `章前言`.
7. `章前言` should focus on what will be done, why, the purpose, and the target.
8. Process wording is suitable for `章小结`, not for `章前言`.
