# Engineering Paper Method Rules

1. When applying a method, explicitly critique the limitations of alternative methods.
2. Every scale, threshold, parameter choice, and setting must have an explicit basis, such as optimization theory, official standards, prior literature, or controlled experiments.
3. If a parameter or scale lacks a clear basis, introduce a sensitivity experiment instead of making a subjective choice.
4. The dimensions, variables, and theoretical constructs proposed earlier must stay aligned with how they are later used; do not change dimensional meaning midway.
5. Weight settings must not be subjective; determine them through experiments, mathematical methods, or explicit optimization procedures.
6. Method writing must prioritize mechanism, design logic, and reproducible settings over self-evaluative language.
