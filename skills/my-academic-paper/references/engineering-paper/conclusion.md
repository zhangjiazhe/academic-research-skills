# Engineering Paper Conclusion Rules

1. Write strictly from the completed-study perspective, looking back at the whole research rather than previewing what will be done.
2. Open the section with the exact large-problem framing logic:
   - `围绕 xx 问题，本文构建了 xx，形成了 xx，以达到 xx 目的。得到的主要结论有：`
3. After that opening sentence, list the core conclusions in ordered form:
   - `（1）一句话总结 + 定量结果`
   - `（2）……`
4. Each conclusion item must stay concise and refined. The body is detailed; the conclusion is a compressed summary of technical route, results, and findings already established in the body.
5. Do not introduce any new term, new result, or new claim. Whatever appears in the conclusion must be directly traceable to the already written body text.
6. If a conclusion involves practical quantitative values, do not overstate precision. Prefer wording such as `约` or `大约` rather than excessively exact engineering numbers.
7. Self-evaluation must remain restrained. Do not exaggerate. Mild wording such as `有效` or `较为准确` is acceptable only after the completed results are already established.
8. Each conclusion item should stay compact, roughly around five rendered lines as a short summary block.
